App Features

LangTek is a focused Spanish to English language learning application designed to make language acquisition intuitive and effective. Our app combines an RSS reader with powerful translation tools, allowing you to learn Spanish through real-world content. With features like word-by-word translation, offline database support, and text-to-speech functionality, LangTek helps you decode the Spanish lexicon.
